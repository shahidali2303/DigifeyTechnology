Our Process

Digifey Technology's web & app development application process

With so many web apps out there, only a few will ever succeed, so it’s important to have a focused and professional application development process.

Ideation

The first step to success is defining your value proposition. We work with you from the initial project planning stage to conduct research and target audience pain points to ensure a concept that satisfies market needs.

Market research

With a product idea in place, gather feedback from your app’s target market to verify its value. This analysis should include the demographics, motivations, behavior patterns, and goals of your buyer persona.

Wireframing

Wireframing helps you to map out the user journey and visualize your end product quickly. It’s a vital preparatory blueprint that will define your app’s structure, content and functionality.

Prototyping

Prototyping enables you to verify your concept prior to software development, gaining feedback from stakeholders and evaluating design.

UI & UX design

With everything sketched out, we’ll get to work on delivering an effortless, intuitive and consistent user interface that delivers a fantastic experience for your target audience.

Development

We take care of front-end and back-end development, with continual testing and quality assurance to deliver a robust, high performance app.

Publishing & maintenance

With the web development done, we’ll configure your app for a successful launch, We also offer ongoing maintenance to fix issues and maintain performance over time.
